This creamy pasta is a quick and simple dish made with simple ingredients. It's rich, flavorful, and perfect for weeknight dinners or for birthday parties!

Ingredients

  • 300g pasta (penne or fettuccine)
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/3 cup grated mozeralla cheese
  • Salt to taste
  • Black pepper to taste
  • Chili flakes (optional)
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Method

  1. Boil the pasta in salted. Drain and set it aside.
  2. Take a pan, heat the olive oil in medium heat.
  3. Add choped garlic and sauté until fragrant (about 1 or 2 minute).
  4. Pour in the heavy cream and let it simmer for 2–3 minutes.
  5. Stir in the mozeralla cheese until it melts and the sauce thickens slightly.
  6. Season with salt, pepper, and chili flakes (if you want).
  7. now mix the cooked pasta to the sauce and mix well until coated.
  8. Cook for another 2 minutes to let the flavors and get it in creamy way.
  9. Garnish with chopped parsley and serve warm.
