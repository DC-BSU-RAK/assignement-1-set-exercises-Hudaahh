Overview

Interstellar is a sci-fi epic directed by Christopher Nolan. It follows a group of astronauts who travel through a wormhole near Saturn in search of a new home for humanity, as Earth becomes increasingly uninhabitable.

Key Information

Director: Christopher Nolan

Release Year: 2014

Genre: Science Fiction, Drama, Adventure

Duration: 2h 49m

Cast & Creative Team

  • Matthew McConaughey as Cooper
  • Anne Hathaway as Amelia Brand
  • Jessica Chastain as Murph
  • Michael Caine as Professor Brand
  • Directed by Christopher Nolan
  • Music by Hans Zimmer

Main Characters

Cooper: A former NASA pilot who leads the space mission to find a new planet.

Murph: Cooper’s brilliant daughter who grows up to solve crucial equations for humanity's survival.

Amelia: A scientist committed to completing her father’s mission.
